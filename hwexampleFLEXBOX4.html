Second article

Driver safety is a critical aspect of responsible driving, involving adherence to traffic laws and defensive driving techniques.

Driving responsibly includes obeying traffic signals, yielding the right-of-way, and following speed limits.

It's important to avoid distractions while driving, such as texting or using a mobile phone, to prevent accidents.
